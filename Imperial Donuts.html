About

Imperial donuts is an independently owned family run business in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in 2014, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our carefully crafted donuts. Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise, and among our popular donuts include the honey-glazed donut, the jam-filled donut and our iced-glazed donut.

Currently, we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily basis and have two stores in the Toronto area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it, - don't worry! We recently introduced both a catering service for special occasions such as weddings & birthdays and also recently introduced a delivery service for our 'surprise Donut collection Box' which contains 6 different Donut flavors.
